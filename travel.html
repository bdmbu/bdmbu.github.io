Discovering the World

I have always loved traveling and discovering new cultures. I am atracted by the different cultures and how they reflect their personalities in their food, clothes and traditions. As a self proclaimed foodie all these things attract me to try out as many dishes as I can when I visit a new city.

One of the best ways I found to get to know a country was to actually live there. I have now lived in the US, Australia, Malta and currently I live in Switzerland. In between I have used my proximity to other countires to visit them over the weekends. In the future I hope to be able to discover Europe through epic road trips!
